# Hair guidance

Use this file whenever hair color or hairstyle is missing, vague, or too generic.

## Principle

Do not default to black hair or the same male haircut just because the user did not specify one.

Choose hair color and hairstyle to support:
- the subject's personality
- the subject's role or social impression
- the overall mood of the portrait
- the house style's clean, youthful, stylized 3D look

Keep the result harmonious and believable. Prefer natural or softly stylized colors unless the user explicitly asks for fantasy hair.

## Hair color mapping

### Sunny, lively, sporty, cheerful, outgoing
Good defaults:
- warm dark blond
- honey blond
- light chestnut
- warm golden brown
- soft caramel brown

Use when the subject reads as:
- 阳光
- 活泼
- 开朗
- 运动感
- 外向
- 少年感 strong but still young-adult

### Gentle, approachable, bookish, soft, warm
Good defaults:
- chestnut brown
- cocoa brown
- medium brown
- soft auburn brown
- warm ash brown

Use when the subject reads as:
- 温柔
- 亲和
- 文静
- 书卷气
- 治愈感

### Cool, sharp, composed, fashionable, modern
Good defaults:
- ash brown
- smoky brown
- espresso brown
- deep neutral brown
- muted platinum or light ash blond if the styling is clearly fashion-forward

Use when the subject reads as:
- 冷感
- 清冷
- 时髦
- 酷
- 利落
- 先锋 but not fantasy

### Professional, calm, reliable, serious
Good defaults:
- deep brown
- espresso
- soft black
- dark ash brown

Use when the subject reads as:
- 专业
- 稳重
- 冷静
- 可靠
- 主播 / 顾问 / 职场角色

### Artistic, playful, eccentric in a restrained way
Good defaults:
- muted copper brown
- dusty auburn
- beige blond
- muted silver-blond
- smoky rose-brown

Only use these when the subject truly invites more character.
Avoid neon, saturated anime colors, or rainbow fashion hair unless the user explicitly asks.

## Hairstyle mapping

### Sunny, lively, sporty young man
Good defaults:
- airy textured crop
- soft tousled short cut
- light side-part with lifted fringe
- relaxed curtain crop with movement

### Gentle, bookish, approachable young man
Good defaults:
- soft medium-short side part
- tidy natural fringe
- lightly tousled layered short hair
- neat soft middle-part if it fits face language

### Cool, modern, stylish young man
Good defaults:
- clean textured crop with shape
- controlled two-block inspired cut softened into the house style
- sleek soft side part
- contemporary curtain-style short cut

### Professional, calm male presentation
Good defaults:
- neat side part
- tidy short cut with subtle texture
- clean combed-back short style with softness
- restrained short layered cut

### Sunny, lively female presentation
Good defaults:
- short bright bob
- airy shoulder-length layers
- soft ponytail silhouette if the crop still reads clearly
- lively medium bob with side sweep

### Cool, modern female presentation
Good defaults:
- asymmetrical bob
- sleek short bob
- layered lob with shape
- short wolf-lite silhouette softened for the house style

### Gentle, warm female presentation
Good defaults:
- soft rounded bob
- medium-length layered hair with calm fringe
- tidy shoulder-length cut with subtle inward curve
- gentle side-swept medium hair

## General haircut rules

- Vary male hairstyles across outputs. Do not reuse one default cut repeatedly.
- Keep all hairstyles simplified into soft sculpted clumps.
- Favor readable silhouette over strand realism.
- Match haircut energy to the character.
- If the user explicitly specifies hair color or hairstyle, preserve it.
- If the user partially specifies one but not the other, infer the missing piece to support the same mood.
- If the subject is bland because of over-safe defaults, the first place to add individuality is hair, before adding more props.
